Approach

At MEL, we believe healthcare should be accessible to everyone, regardless of location or individual circumstances. We offer a wide range of medical services, including teleconsultations and medical counseling, chronic prescription renewals and complementary remote diagnostic methods, for patients from 0 to 100 years old.

We also provide in-person services, including home visits within a 100 km radius of Lisbon, to meet our patients' immediate healthcare needs.
